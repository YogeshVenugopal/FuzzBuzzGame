BULLS or COWS

AI vs Human · Guess the secret number

📋

HOW TO PLAY

01
Think of a 4-digit secret number with ALL UNIQUE DIGITS (no repeats). Example: 2000 ✗ (has three 0s), 2947 ✓ (all different)
02
You guess first. Each round alternates: Human → AI → Human…
03
After each guess, you give feedback:
🐂 Bull = correct digit, correct position
🐄 Cow = correct digit, wrong position
Example: Secret is 2947 → You guess 2000 → 1 Bull (first digit), 0 Cows
04
The AI discovers your number purely through feedback. Be honest with your bull/cow counts — the AI has no other way to know!
05
4 Bulls = WIN! 🎉 First player to crack the code takes the crown!
